# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 18, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Netmsha ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and password
- **Profile Information**: Country, timezone, and profile photo (optional)
- **Session Data**: Walk sessions, schedules, and messages with your partner
- **Device Information**: Push notification tokens and crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the App
- To enable partner pairing and walk sessions
- To send push notifications
- To manage subscriptions
- To improve the App

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

- **Supabase** - Database and authentication ([Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Firebase** - Push notifications and analytics ([Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy))
- **RevenueCat** - Subscription management ([Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy))

Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

## Data Security

Your data is transmitted securely using encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on secure cloud servers.

## Data Retention

Session history is retained for 7 days (Free) or indefinitely (Premium). Account data is retained until you delete your account.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy and will post changes here.
